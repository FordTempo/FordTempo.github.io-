Conceptualization

The idea was to build a game where you would

get access to various vehicles to drive around a city

Planning


various buildings and various types of vehicles were planned

From normal civillian cars to heavy duty semi trucks

A system where you could earn money by driving is planned

including damage to cars to act realism for players






Development


Many buildings were modeled individually with their very own interiors

This was to give players more interactivity playing each role in the city

For materials to use, i used materials and textures that would be seen on real buildings
