Game Designs




Conceptualization


These are the sketches and drawings made that inspired my ideas


The idea was to build a game where you could

damage buses heavily inspired by the public buses of Singapore.


There is going to be a arena where the actual damaging between players will happen.

Lastly, i included a area where you could spawn the buses to damage.





Planning


Diff types of buses were considered

From Articulated buses to single floor buses

A point system was planned to be implemented

Adding some slopes and jumps in the arean to enhance the damaging experience







Development


Many internal parts of the buses were modeled from individual parts

This is to allow for players to get more interactivity with damaging the buses

For materials to use, ii tried to replicate as best as i could to real buses

For scale of the buses, i made sure they are scaled to the average character>/p>
