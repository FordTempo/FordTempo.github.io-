My Childhood

Joining Science Club in Primary school made me discover a world of possibilities and learning about science further propelled my interest into discovery.

As i continued exploring, i joined media club in secondary school to get myself in-tuned with the technical and digital side of this world of information technology.

Being in Media Club


From the technicality of the audio mixer to the complexity of a good photograph. Media club really widen my eyes and allowed me to showcase and hone my skills in the artistic field.
