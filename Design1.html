Bus Designs




Conceptualization


These are the sketches and drawings made that inspired my idea

The idea was to replicate as much as i could to the real bus.

The doors and lights will have interior buttons to enable

The desto will also have a seperate function within the bus





Planning


After conceptualization, I used videos and pictures of the bus

to help me identify several key components to the function of the bus

Firstly, the orientation of how the doors open

Secondly, the change reflected on the desto when a route is selected

Thirdly, How the lights illuminate under darkness






Development


After planning, it was time to develop the model

Details like the material of the interior and material of the bus itself were considered

The height, width and length of the bus was calculated based on the

actual dimensions of the bus compared to the dimension of a stud on roblox studios

Scania K230UB Euro IV Batch 1

Length in real life:12 Meter

Width in real life:2.55 Meter

Height in real life:3.22 Meter
